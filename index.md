# Privacy Policy for ChippyMan Pro

Last updated: 12 February 2026

ChippyMan Pro ("the App") is developed and operated by VibeTribeStudio ("we", "us", or "our").

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

---

## 1. Overview

ChippyMan Pro is a professional tool designed for carpenters and tradespeople. The App is intended for business use and does not require users to create an account.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how data is handled.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Enter Into the App

ChippyMan Pro allows users to input job-related information, which may include:

- Job details  
- Measurements  
- Notes  
- Customer names or addresses (if entered by the user)  
- Pricing information  

This data is stored locally on your device.

We do not access, collect, or transmit this data to our servers.

---

### 2.2 Subscription and Payments

ChippyMan Pro offers a subscription via Google Play.

All payment processing is handled securely by Google Play. We do not receive or store:

- Credit card details  
- Bank account details  
- Billing addresses  

We may receive basic subscription status information from Google Play (such as whether a subscription is active) in order to enable Pro features.

For more information, please refer to:  
https://policies.google.com/privacy

---

### 2.3 Automatically Collected Information

The App does not:

- Collect analytics data  
- Use tracking technologies  
- Use advertising SDKs  
- Collect device location  
- Access contacts or media without your action  

Standard technical data may be processed by Google Play as part of app distribution and billing.

---

## 3. Data Storage

All data entered into the App is stored locally on your device.

You are responsible for managing backups of your data if applicable.

We do not store or back up your job data on external servers.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

The only third-party service involved is Google Play, which handles:

- App distribution  
- Subscription billing  
- License verification  

---

## 5. Data Security

Because data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device’s security settings.

We recommend:

- Using device-level security (PIN, password, biometrics)  
- Keeping your device updated  

---

## 6. Your Rights (UK & EU Users)

If you are located in the UK or EU, you have rights under applicable data protection laws, including:

- The right to access personal data  
- The right to request deletion  
- The right to restrict processing  

As we do not collect or store personal data on external servers, most data control is managed directly on your device.

If you have questions, you may contact us at:

vtribestudio@gmail.com

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

ChippyMan Pro is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Updates will be posted at the URL provided in the Google Play listing.
